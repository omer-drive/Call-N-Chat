🚀 GETTING STARTED
1
Open this page and type your name to enter the room.
2
Copy the link (🔗 COPY LINK button top right) and send it to your friend.
3
Your friend opens the link and types their name. You're now in the same room.
4
Their Peer ID card auto-appears in 💬 Chat. Click 📹 VIDEO or 🎙 VOICE on it to call them.
5
They'll see an incoming call popup — they hit ANSWER and you're connected!
📱 WHAT EACH TAB DOES
💬
CHAT
Real-time messages. Both of you see them instantly. Type + hit Enter.
📞
CALL
Video or voice call. Paste 1 ID for 1-on-1, or 2 IDs for a 3-way call!
🎲
DICE
Roll dice together. Pick d6 or d20. Results auto-post in chat.
⬜
TTT
Tic-Tac-Toe vs your friend. Takes turns automatically. Tracks wins.
🪙
COIN
Flip a coin. Result shared in chat. Shows flip history.
🖥
SCREEN
During a call, hit 🖥 SCREENSHARE to share your screen with your friend.
🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING
❌
Friend can't see messages? Make sure you're both on the exact same URL. Must be HTTPS (GitHub Pages), not a local file.
❌
Camera/mic not working? Click Allow when the browser asks. Only works on HTTPS. Try Chrome or Edge.
❌
Call not connecting? Peer IDs change on every refresh — always call from the chat card, not a saved ID.
❌
On school/work WiFi? Some networks block calls. Try switching to mobile data.
✅
Status bar at top shows 🟢 CONNECTED when Firebase is ready. If it's red, check your internet.
