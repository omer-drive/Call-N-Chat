Help
How to use Omer 'N Chat
Getting started

Signing up

  1. Enter a display name, email, and password
  2. Check your inbox and click the verification link
  3. Come back and press "I've verified — let me in"

Your display name is permanent

It's locked in when you first sign up — this prevents impersonation and keeps things honest.

Chat & groups

Messages

Press Enter to send, Shift+Enter for a new line. Hover any message to react. URLs auto-link.

Groups

Click the ＋ next to Groups in the sidebar. Give it a name and pick members. Group chats work the same as #general but are private to the members you chose.

Direct messages

Click anyone's name in the Online list or hover and click "Direct Message". The DM panel slides in from the right.

Calls

Starting a call

  1. Go to Voice & Video
  2. Find their Peer ID card in chat
  3. Click Video or Voice
  4. They accept and you're connected

Troubleshooting

  • No audio from caller? Click anywhere on the page first — browsers require user interaction before playing audio
  • Can't connect? Peer IDs reset on reload — get a fresh one from #general
  • On school/work WiFi? WebRTC may be blocked — try mobile data
  • Camera/mic blocked? Click the lock icon in your URL bar and allow access
