Help & Guide ❓
Everything you need to use the app
🚀 Getting Started

How to join

  1. Get an invite link (🔗 button in sidebar)
  2. Sign up with email or Google
  3. Email users: verify your email first
  4. You're in — say hi in #general! 👋

🔒 Display name is permanent

Your name is locked when you first sign up. This keeps things honest — pick something you'll like!

💬 Chat

Sending messages

Type and press Enter to send. Shift+Enter for new line. Click 😊 for emoji. URLs auto-link.

Reactions

Hover a message → click + to react. Click a reaction again to add or remove yours.

📞 Calls

Starting a call

  1. Go to 🎙 Voice & Video
  2. Find their Peer ID card in #general
  3. Click 📹 Video or 🎙 Voice on the card
  4. They'll get a popup — once they answer, you're connected!

Troubleshooting

  • No audio? Click anywhere first — browsers block audio until you interact
  • Can't connect? Peer IDs reset on reload — grab a fresh one from chat
  • School WiFi? Try mobile data — some networks block WebRTC
  • Camera blocked? Click the lock icon in your browser URL bar
