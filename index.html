How to use Nexus

Your private communication hub — chat, call, and DM in one place.

🚀 Getting started
1. Share the page URL with your friends (🔗 in the sidebar)
2. Everyone opens the link and enters their name
3. Your Peer ID auto-posts in #general — friends click it to call you
4. Chat in #general or send private DMs by clicking a username
📞 Calls (up to 4 people)
Go to 🎙 Voice & Video in the sidebar. Paste up to 3 friend Peer IDs. Hit Video or Voice — it calls everyone at once. When someone calls you, a popup appears bottom-right.
💌 Private Messages
Click any username in the Online list or in chat to open a DM panel on the right side. Only you and that person can see it (stored with a private key in Firebase).
🔧 Troubleshooting
No audio in call? Click anywhere on the page — browser needs a click to allow audio.
Call not connecting? Peer IDs change on refresh — always use the chat card.
On school WiFi? Try mobile data — some networks block WebRTC.
Status bar red? Check Firebase rules are set to allow read/write.
