Help & Guide ❓
Everything you need to know to use the app
🚀 Getting Started

How to join

  1. Get an invite link from someone already in the chat
  2. Sign up with email, or just use Google (easier!)
  3. If you used email, verify it first by clicking the link we sent
  4. You're in — go say hi in #general! 👋

🔒 Your display name is permanent

When you first sign up, we lock your display name in for good. This is intentional — it keeps things honest and stops impersonation. Pick something you'll be happy with!

🔗 Inviting people

Click the 🔗 button in the top-left sidebar header to copy the invite link. Paste it in a text, email, Discord — wherever!

💬 Chat

Sending messages

Type in the box at the bottom and hit Enter to send. For a new line, use Shift+Enter. Click 😊 for the emoji picker. URLs get auto-linked!

😄 Reacting to messages

Hover over any message to see the reactions row. Click the + to pick an emoji reaction. Click an existing reaction to add yours, or click it again to remove it.

✍️ Typing indicators

When someone's typing, you'll see their name with bouncing dots at the bottom. They disappear automatically 3 seconds after they stop typing.

💌 Direct Messages

How to DM someone

Click anyone's name in the Online list or in the chat — the DM panel slides in from the right. It's fully private, just the two of you.

📞 Calls

Starting a call

  1. Go to 🎙 Voice & Video in the sidebar
  2. Find their Peer ID card in #general chat
  3. Click 📹 Video or 🎙 Voice on their card
  4. They get a popup — once they answer, you're connected!

Call controls

  • 🎤 Mute — toggle your mic
  • 📷 Cam — toggle your camera
  • 🖥 Share Screen — share your screen (must be in a call first)
  • 📵 End Call — hang up

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • No audio? Click anywhere on the page — browsers block audio until you interact
  • Can't connect? Peer IDs reset on page reload — grab a fresh one from #general
  • School/office WiFi? Some networks block WebRTC. Try mobile data or a hotspot
  • Camera/mic blocked? Click the lock icon in your browser's URL bar and allow access
⚙️ How It Works

Under the hood

Chat and presence use Firebase Firestore for real-time sync. Calls are peer-to-peer via PeerJS/WebRTC — nobody's server ever sees your audio or video. Accounts use Firebase Auth.
