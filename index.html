Help & Guide ❓
Everything you need to know
🚀 Getting Started

Signing up

  1. Enter your email and a password
  2. Check your inbox — we'll send a verification link
  3. Click the link in the email
  4. Come back and press "I clicked the link — let me in!"
  5. You're in! Say hi in #general 👋

🔒 Display name is permanent

Your name gets locked in when you first sign up — you can't change it later. This keeps things honest and stops impersonation.

💬 Chat

Sending messages

Type in the box and press Enter to send. Use Shift+Enter for a new line. Click 😊 for emojis. URLs auto-link!

😄 Reactions

Hover any message and click the + to react with an emoji. Click an existing reaction to add yours too.

💌 DMs

Direct messages

Click anyone's name in the Online list or in chat. The DM panel slides in from the right. Fully private — just you two.

📞 Calls

Starting a call

  1. Go to 🎙 Voice & Video
  2. Find their Peer ID card in #general
  3. Click 📹 Video or 🎙 Voice
  4. They accept and you're connected!

Troubleshooting

  • No audio? Click anywhere on the page first — browsers require a user interaction before playing audio
  • Can't connect? Peer IDs reset on reload — grab a fresh one from #general
  • School WiFi? Some networks block WebRTC — try mobile data
  • Camera blocked? Click the lock icon in your URL bar and allow access
