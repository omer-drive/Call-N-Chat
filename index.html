Help & Guide ❓
Everything you need to know about Omer 'N Chat
🚀 Getting Started

📋 How to join

  1. Someone shares this page's URL with you
  2. Create a free account with email or Google
  3. Verify your email (check spam if it doesn't arrive!)
  4. You're in — say hi in #general!

🔒 About your display name

Your display name is set once when you create your account, and it's stored permanently to your profile. There's no way to change it — this keeps things honest and prevents impersonation. Choose something you like!

🔗 Inviting friends

Click the 🔗 icon in the sidebar header to copy the invite link to your clipboard. Send it to whoever you want to join. Anyone with the link can make an account.

💬 Chatting

📝 Sending messages

Type in the box at the bottom of #general and hit Enter to send. Press Shift + Enter for a new line. Use the 😊 button to open the emoji picker.

😄 Reactions

Hover over any message to see action buttons. Click the emoji button to react. Reactions show up below the message — click one to add yours to the pile!

✍️ Typing indicators

When someone is typing, you'll see their name with animated dots at the bottom of the chat. They'll see yours too when you're typing!

💌 Direct Messages

How to DM someone

Click any username in the Online list on the left, or click a name in the chat. A DM panel slides in from the right. Only you and that person can see those messages.

📞 Voice & Video Calls

Starting a call

  1. Go to 🎙 Voice & Video in the sidebar
  2. Find the other person's Peer ID card in #general
  3. Click 📹 Video or 🎙 Voice on their card — or paste their ID manually
  4. When they answer, you're connected!

📋 Call controls

  • 🎤 Mute — toggle your microphone on/off
  • 📷 Cam — toggle your camera on/off
  • 🖥 Share — share your screen (requires active call)
  • 📵 End — hang up the call

🔧 Troubleshooting calls

  • No audio? Click anywhere on the page — browsers block audio until you interact
  • Can't connect? Peer IDs reset on page refresh — always grab the latest one from chat
  • On school/work WiFi? Some networks block WebRTC. Try mobile data
  • Camera not working? Check browser permissions (click the lock icon in your URL bar)
⚙️ Technical Info

How it works

Chat messages and presence are stored in Firebase Firestore (real-time sync). Voice/video calls are peer-to-peer using PeerJS/WebRTC — Omer's server never sees your call audio or video. Auth is handled by Firebase Auth.

Privacy

Messages are stored in Firestore. DMs use a private key based on both usernames. Nobody outside your pair can read them unless they have Firestore admin access.
